Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 22, 2026

Act Two Solutions LLC ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website or mobile applications.

1. Information We Collect

Our philosophy is simple: we do not collect personal information unless it is necessary to provide our services.

  • Personal Information: We do not collect names, addresses, or phone numbers within our applications unless you explicitly provide them (e.g., via a support email or contact form).

If you contact us via email, we use your email address solely to reply to your inquiry. We do not sell, rent, or share your contact information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

Our applications may rely on third-party services for core functionality (such as Apple iCloud for data syncing or Firebase for backend services). These services have their own privacy policies and may process data as described in their respective terms.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
