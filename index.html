Our Mission

Based in Wisconsin, Act Two Solutions LLC develops specialized software that leverages large language models (LLMs) to solve complex data challenges. We focus on sectors where massive amounts of unstructured data—such as case files, transcripts, and records—create bottlenecks for human investigators. Our goal is to provide clarity and actionable intelligence through secure, intuitive digital products.

Flagship Product: Forensic-Analysis

CURRENT STAGE: MVP / PRIVATE BETA

AI-Powered Cold Case Intelligence

The Problem: Law enforcement agencies often have thousands of pages of digitized cold case files. Manually cross-referencing names, dates, and locations across decades of paperwork is nearly impossible.

The Solution: Our Forensic-Analysis tool uses a private, secure RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) architecture to allow investigators to "talk" to their case files. It identifies patterns, generates timelines, and surfaces inconsistencies in witness statements automatically.

  • Status: Currently in early-stage stakeholder review with regional law enforcement partners.
