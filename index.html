The Intelligence Dashboard

Our platform transforms massive volumes of unstructured investigative data into actionable insights using a secure, private RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) architecture.

  • Seamless Case Import: Easily ingest thousands of pages of digitized case files, reports, and transcripts.
  • Automated Master Timelines: Automatically extract dates and events to build interactive, chronological investigation maps.
  • Evidence Cataloging: Instant identification and categorization of physical and digital evidence items.
  • Entity & Relationship Mapping: Identify People of Interest and visualize complex social and criminal relationships across documents.
  • Deep Statement Analysis: Cross-reference witness statements to identify inconsistencies or corroborating patterns.
  • "Interrogate" the File: Chat directly with your case files using Detective AI to find specific answers in seconds.
