The Product: Forensic-Analysis

Speed to Insight

A single case file can easily exceed 2,000 pages of reports, transcripts, and evidence logs. Human investigators face a significant cognitive burden: it is nearly impossible to recall a subtle witness conflict on page 27 when you are currently analyzing page 2,027.

Forensic-Analysis eliminates these gaps. By instantly cross-referencing every word in a case file, our platform surfaces inconsistencies, corroborates statements, and brings new detectives up to speed in minutes rather than weeks.

Anonymized Interface: Identifying patterns across 30+ years of case data.

Automated Workflows

Stop manual logging. The platform automatically generates comprehensive Master Timelines and Evidence Lists directly from case documentation, allowing investigators to focus on strategy rather than clerical work.

Interrogate the File

Use "Detective AI" to ask complex questions like "Where was the suspect during the 48-hour window before the incident?" Receive instant answers with Tap-to-Verify functionality that opens the exact source page in the case file.

The Investigator that Never Rests

The platform continually monitors case files in the background. If a new forensic technology (like a modern lab test) becomes relevant to an old piece of evidence, the system alerts the investigator immediately.

Enterprise Security

Built on CJIS-compliant infrastructure, our solution ensures that sensitive data remains encrypted and sovereign. We utilize trusted, enterprise-grade AI models (Google Vertex AI) that never train on your private data.
